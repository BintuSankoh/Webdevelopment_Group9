News and Events

Exam Results Partially Published — Further Delays Expected

Posted 17 Apr 2026 · Examinations Office

Following an earlier delay to the release of semester results, the Examinations Office has confirmed that a number of results are now available to view on the student portal. Remaining results are still being processed and are expected to be published within the next two weeks. Students are asked to check their portal regularly for updates.

Summer Exam Timetables Now Live

Posted 15 Apr 2026 · Examinations Office

The summer examination timetable has been published on the student portal. All students should review their scheduled dates and times and report any clashes or conflicts to the Examinations Office no later than 25 April.

Archery Club Open Day — New Members Welcome

Posted 14 Apr 2026 · Student Union

The college Archery Club is hosting an open day on 26 April for students interested in joining. No experience is needed — equipment is provided and qualified instructors will be on hand. The club competes in regional tournaments and meets every Wednesday evening. Sign up at the Student Union desk or drop in on the day.

Drama Society Seeking New Members Ahead of Summer Production

Posted 12 Apr 2026 · Student Union

The Drama Society is holding open auditions and crew sign-ups for their summer production. Whether you're interested in performing, set design, lighting, or stage management, all are welcome. Auditions take place on 29 April in the Performing Arts Studio.

Library Extended Hours for Exam Season

Posted 11 Apr 2026 · Library Services

From 28 April, the main library will remain open until midnight to support students during the exam period. Study room bookings can be made through the library portal up to three days in advance.
